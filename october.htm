THE DUTCH Clojure EVENT

October Amsterdam Clojure is the Dutch stage for functional geeks, lots of parenthesis and fun, all in a spicy LISP sauce!
The 2012 edition will be held at Backbase Headquarters in Amsterdam on Saturday October 27th.

As always, the event is FREE to attend!

Submit your talk

HOW TO JOIN

The event is completely free for anyone to join. Unfortunately, space limitations requires us to ask you to sign up. The official registration form will soon be available on our Meetup page.

Be sure to book your seat or you'll seriously risk to miss the hottest event of this Dutch fall!
